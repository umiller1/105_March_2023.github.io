Duck Sauce

Animal: Doggo

Breed: Corgi

Age: 250 yrs

Temperament: Sanguine

Coat: Brown and White

Vaccines: Yes

Meet Duck Sauce, the canine connoisseur of human interaction! This four-legged socialite is more people-oriented than a neighborhood block party. Duck Sauce's favorite hobby? Crashing picnics with a wagging tail and a charming grin, turning every outdoor gathering into a full-blown petting zoo. With a knack for sniffing out new friends (and the occasional hidden treat), this pup has mastered the art of making humans feel like the most fascinating creatures on Earth. Duck Sauce's secret weapon? The hypnotic power of those soulful, puppy-dog eyes that can convince even the grumpiest of neighbors to share their snacks. When he's not busy charming the pants off everyone he meets, Duck Sauce enjoys long walks in the park, where he can continue his mission of transforming strangers into loyal belly-rub providers. So, if you're in need of a furry friend who's as people-oriented as they come, Duck Sauce is your go-to canine companion. Just be prepared to have your heart stolen (and your snacks pilfered) by this lovable social butterfly!
