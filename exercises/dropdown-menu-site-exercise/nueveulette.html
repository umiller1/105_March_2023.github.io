Nueveulette

Animal: Birb

Breed: Toucan?

Age: 14 yrs

Temperament: Rowdy

Meet Nueveulette, the melodramatic toucan with a flair for nighttime performances! While most birds are known for their early-morning chirps, Nueveulette has flipped the script and turned 3am into her stage time. Armed with a beak that could rival a saxophone's soulful tones, she serenades the moon with the most heart-wrenching, tear-inducing, and downright melodramatic love songs you've ever heard. Whether you're a night owl or not, Nueveulette's midnight concert series is a unique experience you won't soon forget. So, if you're in need of a feathered friend who can turn your sleepless nights into a symphony of sadness and romance, Nueveulette the toucan is ready to croon her way into your heart and your dreams. Just make sure to have a tissue handy for those 3am sobfests!
